Hobbies & Interests

Gaming

I have a deep passion for gaming that has evolved over the years. When I was younger, I was eager to enjoy rhythm games like Geometry Dash, osu!, and Piano Tiles. As I grew older, my interests shifted towards MOBA, FPS, and story-driven games like God of War and Genshin Impact.

Web Development

I’m also passionate about web development. I’ve been developing websites mainly for university activities and projects, working with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I also explore frameworks such as Laravel and React to build more dynamic and efficient web applications.

Music

Music has always been a huge part of my life. I love listening to emo, punk rock, and metal bands that bring energy and emotion to every track. Some of my favorites include My Chemical Romance, Bring Me The Horizon, Slipknot, and Linkin Park. Their music fuels my creativity, whether I’m coding, gaming, or just unwinding. There’s nothing like a good playlist to set the mood!

PC Building

Building and customizing PCs is something I really enjoy. I love researching the best components, optimizing performance, and assembling rigs that suit different needs—whether it’s for gaming, development, or productivity. There's something satisfying about picking the perfect parts and seeing everything come together. I’m always on the lookout for new hardware trends and ways to get the most out of a build!
